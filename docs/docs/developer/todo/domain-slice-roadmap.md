# Domain Slice Roadmap

This page tracks additional domain slices to consider beyond the current baseline.

## Current Baseline

- Auth
- Platform
- GRC
- IDM
- ITAM
- DDI

## Candidate Slices

### Now

- Service and Application Portfolio
- Vulnerability and Exposure Management
- Data Governance and Classification

### Next

- Incident and Problem Management
- Change and Release Management
- Third-Party and Vendor Risk
- Secrets and Key Management

### Later

- Endpoint and Configuration Baselines
- Workflow and Case Management
- Knowledge and Policy Operations

## Prioritization Notes

- Prioritize slices that improve risk evidence quality for GRC first.
- Prefer slices that directly enrich ownership, criticality, and dependency modeling.
- Keep each slice domain-model focused (entities, relationships, lifecycle, invariants).
