# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful community for everyone contributing to ITSoR.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
- Assume good intent and provide constructive feedback.
- Focus discussions on technical issues and project outcomes.
- Be considerate of different experience levels and backgrounds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Threatening, abusive, or demeaning language.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without explicit consent.
- Any conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional collaboration setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Community interactions in project-related channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to repository maintainers.

When reporting, include:

- What happened
- Where/when it happened
- Any relevant links or screenshots

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- Warning the participant
- Removing comments or content
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation

Enforcement decisions are made to protect the project and community.
